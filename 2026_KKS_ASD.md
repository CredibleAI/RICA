# Automatic Autism Spectrum Disorder detection based on amateur videos with children

**Mentor**: Dr. [Kamil Książek](https://scholar.google.pl/citations?user=i0N4lY8AAAAJ&hl=en&oi=ao)

The main goal of the project is an early identification of Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) based on amateur videos, supporting the early therapy implementation. It is expected to prepare an artificial intelligence algorithm pipeline for the analysis of videos with children. It includes object detection and 3D human pose estimation, and further analysis of the position and movement of individual human body parts (head, hands, feet, etc.) in time. Finally, a classifier / a regression model will be prepared. Additionally, an explanation of the model decision should be performed (which movement schemes correspond to typical autism behaviour).

Initially, an object detection / pose estimation model selection will be made based on public videos. Then, a more detailed analysis of the selected model operation will be done on a larger number of samples, including videos from our partners in the future. During the project, the potential of the fusion model will also be considered, combining the movement and voice of children.
